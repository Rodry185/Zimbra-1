Recovering deleted items

The Trash folder is available for each Zimbra application you use. Only items specific to the selected application are displayed when you open the Trash folder from the application. But when you select Empty Trash from any application, items from all applications are deleted.

Before you empty the trash folder, you can recover files from the Trash by dragging and dropping them into another folder.

Messages left in the Zimbra Trash may be automatically purged, in which case you cannot recover them. Messages left in your other accounts' Trash folders are not deleted automatically, these messages are retained until you empty the Trash folder.
