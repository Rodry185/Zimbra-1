Restoring Accounts from Your Backup Files

If you have backed up your accounts on a regular basis, it is easy to restore your accounts to the point of the last backup.

To restore your accounts

  1. Go to the Preferences>Backups page. The backup files that are available are listed In the Restore from Backup section.

  2. Check the Active box for the accounts you want to restore.

  3. Click Restore from Backup.  The selected accounts are restored.  Depending on the size of the file, this may take a while to restore the account.  

Do not close Zimbra Desktop while your accounts are being restored.
