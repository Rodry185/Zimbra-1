Turning Off Automatic Sync

Note:  Turning sync off is not available for Zimbra folders.

When you set up accounts to download to the Zimbra Desktop, you set synchronization settings to either sync all your folders in your account or only your Inbox. If you set up to sync all your folders, you can later turn sync off for your personal folders.  

  1. Right-click a folder.

  2. On the menu that displays, elect Turn sync off.

When you turn sync off and then move messages from your Inbox to a folder that is not synced back to your account, the moved messages are removed from the server. Moving messages between folders that are not synced has no impact on the folders in your account.

To sync those folders again, turn sync on. The next time your Zimbra Desktop syncs to your account, the contents of the folder is sync'd.
