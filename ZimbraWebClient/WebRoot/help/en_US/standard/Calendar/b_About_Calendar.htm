About @CompanyName@ Calendar

The @CompanyName@ Calendar lets you track and schedule your appointments, meetings, and events.

Calendar includes the following features:

  • Create different calendars

  • Subscribe to calendars

  • Link to shared calendars

  • Create appointments, schedule all day events, and schedule meetings

  • Accept/Tentative/Reject an invitation with one click

  • View your calendar by various views, including day, 5-day work week, 7-day week, and month

  • View multiple calendars to see potential scheduling conflicts

  • Import and export calendars   

To open the calendar view, select Calendar tab. The Work Week view is the default and is displayed when the calendar is opened the first time.  

You can change which view is displayed when Calendar is opened from the Preference>Calendar tab.

You can change your view from the Calendar toolbar, to see activities for a day, work week, week, month to today.
