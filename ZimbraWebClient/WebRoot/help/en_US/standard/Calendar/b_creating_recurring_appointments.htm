Creating recurring meetings

Recurring meetings are appointments that repeat on some sort of schedule. You can schedule recurring meetings to repeat daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly. In addition, you can customize recurring appointment schedules.

  1. When you create a new appointment, click Repeat on the toolbar to open the recurring appointments configuration page.

  2. In the Basic Repeat section, click the radial button to select how often you want the meeting to be repeated - every day, week, month, or year.

  3. If your recurring meeting has an end date, in the Advanced section End box, select either to end the meetings after a specific number of meetings or by a specific date.

  4. The Advanced section lets you schedule more specific patterns.

    • Daily. Customize daily repeats to choose whether it is every day or some other sequence such as every two days or four days.

    • Weekly. Customize weekly repeats to select whether it is a weekly recurring appointment or some other weekly pattern such as every two weeks.  You can select which day of the week you want the meeting.

    • Monthly. Customize monthly repeats to select whether it is a monthly recurring appointment or some other monthly pattern such as every two months.  A calendar displays and you can select which day or days you want the meeting scheduled.

    • Yearly. Customize yearly repeats to select the month and date.

  1. Click Done to return to the appointment page.
