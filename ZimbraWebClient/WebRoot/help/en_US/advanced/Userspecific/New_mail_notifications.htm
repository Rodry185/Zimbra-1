Email notification to another address

You can configure your mail options to be notified at another email address when you receive mail to your @ClientNameShort@ mailbox.  This feature may be useful if you do not receive a lot of mail to your @CompanyName@ account, but when you do receive mail, it's important enough to require a timely response.

To set notification:

  1. Click the Preferences tab.

  2. Click Mail.

  3. In the Receiving Messages section, check Send a notification message to and enter the email address.

  4. Click Save. The notification feature is enabled immediately.
