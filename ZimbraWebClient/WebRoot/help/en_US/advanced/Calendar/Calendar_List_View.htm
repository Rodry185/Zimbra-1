Calendar List View

The List view in Calendar gives you the following details that are not available in the other Calendar view.

  • Calendar appointments for a specific period of time.

  • Meetings from all selected calendars are listed in a vertical line by date and time, showing:

    • Subject of the meeting

    • Location of the meeting

    • Status, whether the meeting is New, Accepted, Tentative

    • Which calendar the meeting is saved to

    • Whether it is recurring or not

    • Date and time of the meeting

  • You can change the columns that are displayed by right-clicking the header and deselecting the columns you do not want to display.

  • You can sort by subject, status, calendar or date columns.

  • You can select multiple local appointment to act upon at once, actions include deleting appointments, moving appointments to a new calendar, or tagging appointments. You can drag and drop selected appointments into other calendars or to a tag.

  • You can search for meetings by subject or location within the two-week list view.

You cannot print a list view.
